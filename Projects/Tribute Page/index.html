Mahendra Singh Dhoni

A Tribute to captain cool

    Here's a quick review of MS life-:

  • Mahendra Singh Dhoni (commonly known as MS Dhoni) was born on 7 July 1981.
  • He captained the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 to 2016.
  • He also captained the Indian Test side from 2008 to 2014.
  • He made his One Day International (ODI) debut in December 2004 against Bangladesh.
  • He played his first Test a year later against Sri Lanka.
  • He is widely regarded as one of the greatest finishers in limited-overs cricket.
  • He is also regarded as one of the best wicket-keepers in world cricket and is known to have very fast hands.
  • He is the only captain who have won all the three ICC trophies.
